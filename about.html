For Artists

Concrete Valley Records is a new student-run club at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. Started in the Spring of 2018, we seek to function just as a small record label would in the new age of music. This means utilizing all forms of media available and staying up-to-date with current trends in the industry.

UMass Amherst's first and only University Record Label. 100% Student-run.

Half Record Label

  • we seek to help student musicians here at UMass succeed and get heard
  • Using social media, connections to other RSOs like WMUA and UPC, and much more, we hope to get these artists out to as many listeners as possible
  • Providing respirces like graphic design and production expertise to musicians

Half Music Magazine

  • CVR Magazine is a creative online (and hopefully someday printed) compilation of ideas and opinions!
  • Student reviews of recent live shows in the Amherst/Northampton area
  • Innovative pieces about the past, present and future state of the music industry 
  • Information about upcoming concerts, albums, or mixtapes!
