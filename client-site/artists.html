Lowkey Ish
Bio

Lowkey ish blends future funk vibes with live instrumentation and experimental sounds to make a unique, undefinable genre.
Lowkey ish has played his fair share of college parties and played opening sets for the likes of Getter and even Fetty Wap. Wielding a guitar and a Traktor s4, Lowkey ish moves crowds like a hurricane while still keeping the vibes laid back and groovy.

Sunshine Brothers Inc.
Bio

On the East Coast, three sunshine brothers are set to release their debut EP ‘The Sunshine Tape” August 2017. Hailing from the DIY music scene of the Pioneer Valley in Massachusetts, Sunshine Brothers Inc. sets free their first full-length after a year of building buzz in basements, bars, and reputable venues around New England. With their two debut singles both featured on BIRP.fm’s July 2017 playlist, Jake, Charles and John- all college students in Massachusetts- have officially set out to bring their unique, warm, and beaming psych-pop to the people, all with their jangly yet driving guitars, deep, sleek bass, commanding drums, icy, panging synths, sonic harmonies, careful lyricism, and arresting pop songwriting.
